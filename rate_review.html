Jade: Rate & Review

Welcome to the Jade Rate & Review project! You should have submitted at least 3 tasks to be eligible for this qualification. If you have not, please go back and submit at least 3 tasks before attempting this R&R.

You are given a submission from another worker for the Jade project. Please read the instructions below and rate each part accordingly.

URL(s)

Ensure that the provided URLs meet the following criteria:

  • Valid SEC EDGAR URL
  • 10-K or 10-Q
  • Report is published on or after October 2023

If any of the three criteria are not met for any of the URLs needed to answer the question, the URLs are deemed invalid.
